Instructor: Parisa Mahjoor

## Grading Breakdown

- Midterm Exam: 25%
- Final Exam: 35%
- Homework and problem sets: 20%
- Quizzes: 10%
- Participation: 10%

## Learning Objectives

By the end of the course, students will be able to calculate price elasticity of demand, interpret total revenue changes, and explain consumer surplus.

## Exam Guidance

The final exam is cumulative, but it focuses heavily on elasticity, welfare analysis, and homework-style calculation problems. Important topics include price elasticity of demand, consumer surplus, producer surplus, and deadweight loss.
