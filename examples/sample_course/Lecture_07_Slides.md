# Elasticity And Revenue

Price elasticity of demand measures responsiveness of quantity demanded to a change in price.

Formula:
Elasticity = percent change in quantity demanded / percent change in price.

If demand is elastic, a price decrease increases total revenue.
If demand is inelastic, a price increase increases total revenue.

# Common Mistakes

Do not confuse a shift in demand with movement along the demand curve.
Remember that elasticity is usually interpreted using absolute value.
